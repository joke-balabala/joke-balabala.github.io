Research

The Shen lab started at anhui Medical University in Hefei since 2023.Our main research interests are biomedical big data analysis and the development of algorithmic tools.Our aim is to decipher mechanisms underlying key biological processes, including several areas listed below:

  • Development of computational biology tools based on deep learning
  • Medical big data analysis and application based on deep learning
